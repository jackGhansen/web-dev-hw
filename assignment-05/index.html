Delicious Egg Salad for Sandwiches

by: Jack G Hansen

Ingredients

- 8 eggs

- 1/4 cup chopped green onion

- 1/2 cup mayonnaise

- 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard

- 1/4 teaspoon paprika

- salt and pepper to taste


For more quick and easy recipes, click here

Directions

-Place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil

- Immediately remove from heat.

- Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.

- Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.

- Place the chopped eggs in a bowl, and stir in the mayonnaise, mustard and green onion.

- Season with salt, pepper and paprika. Stir and serve on your favorite bread or crackers.
